About Us

We all know these are tough times for everyone and people who are isolating at their homes can use a little help from us to make their life a little better.

A little bit of help from all of us would surely bring a smile on the face of the people in need and would put you in the good books of the one sitting up there.

  • Register yourself as a quarantined person who needs help with food and other necessities.
  • Take the role of volunteer and supply the required items to the needy people.
